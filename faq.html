Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Pixaroid's free image tools.

General

No account or sign-up is required. Open any tool and start processing immediately.
Pixaroid works on all modern browsers: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, and Edge 90+. JavaScript must be enabled.

Privacy & Security

Never. All processing happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API and Web Workers. Your files never leave your device and are never transmitted to any server.
Yes. Since everything runs locally in your browser, confidential photos, documents, and business images never leave your device. Pixaroid has no server-side access to your content.
Pixaroid uses one first-party cookie (pxn-theme) to remember your dark/light mode preference. We do not use tracking cookies. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Formats & File Sizes

Pixaroid supports JPEG, PNG, WebP, HEIC/HEIF (iPhone), GIF, BMP, TIFF, AVIF, and PDF (for conversion tools).
Most tools accept files up to 20 MB per file. Bulk tools handle up to 100 images per batch. Very large files may process more slowly on low-end devices.
Yes. Use the HEIC to JPG or HEIC to PNG converter. HEIC decoding support depends on your browser — Chrome and Safari handle it best.

AI Tools

AI tools use ONNX Runtime Web to run machine learning models directly in your browser. The model is downloaded once and cached locally. All inference runs on your device — no cloud API calls.
The background remover works best on images with clear subject-background contrast. Complex backgrounds with similar colours to the subject may require manual refinement.

Offline & PWA

Yes. Pixaroid is a Progressive Web App (PWA). After your first visit, the service worker caches the app. You can install it via "Add to Home Screen" in your browser and use most tools offline.
On Chrome: click the install icon in the address bar (or menu → "Install Pixaroid"). On Safari (iOS): tap Share → "Add to Home Screen". The app will appear on your home screen like a native app.
