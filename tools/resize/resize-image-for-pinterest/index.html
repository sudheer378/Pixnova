How to Use
  1. Upload your photo by dragging it onto the drop zone or clicking Browse.
  2. Select the Pinterest format you need from the dropdown — Standard Pin (1000×1500) is the most common.
  3. Choose a Fit Mode: Cover fills the frame completely (may crop edges); Contain fits the entire image (may add letterbox).
  4. Click Resize to apply the selected dimensions.
  5. Download your resized image — ready to upload directly to Pinterest.
